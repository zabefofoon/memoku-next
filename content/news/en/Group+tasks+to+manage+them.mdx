As you create tasks, they can become hard to manage.
Memoku reduces the complexity of task management by categorizing tasks with tags and
letting you add related subtasks.
<hr/>
1. Add tags  
Add tags on the settings page.

<img 
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl | border border-gray-300 dark:border-zinc-600' 
    src='/images/contents/groups/group_tags.webp' 
    alt=''
/>

2. Apply tags to tasks  
On the list page, click the tag area of a task and select your tags.

<img 
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl | border border-gray-300 dark:border-zinc-600' 
    src='/images/contents/groups/group_card.webp' 
    alt=''
/>

3. Add subtasks  
Click the [+ Add subtask] area to register a subtask. 

<img 
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl | border border-gray-300 dark:border-zinc-600' 
    src='/images/contents/groups/group_card2.webp' 
    alt=''
/>
