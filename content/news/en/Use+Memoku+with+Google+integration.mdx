Automatically save the tasks you write to Google Sheets and use them across devices.
When you connect Google in Memoku, your data is uploaded to Google automatically.

For example, you can write tasks on your PC and check them on mobile.
Or check tasks written on mobile on your PC as well.
<hr/>
1. Sign in with Google  
Turn on Google integration on the settings page.

<img 
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl | border border-gray-300 dark:border-zinc-600' 
    src='/images/contents/google/google_link.webp' 
    alt=''
/>

2. Create a task  
On the task creation page, write your task.

<img 
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl | border border-gray-300 dark:border-zinc-600' 
    src='/images/contents/google/google_link2.webp' 
    alt=''
/>

3. Check on another device  
On another device, sign in to Google with the same email and check your data.

<img 
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl | border border-gray-300 dark:border-zinc-600' 
    src='/images/contents/google/google_link3.webp' 
    alt=''
/>
<hr/>
*Your data is stored in your Google Drive, so it's secure.*  
*Please make sure the MEMOKU file stored in Google Drive is not deleted!*
