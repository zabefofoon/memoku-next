Without a complicated installation process, add it to your home screen to access Memoku easily.
Enjoy a native-app-like experience on desktop and mobile.

<details>
  <summary className='underline'>Desktop/Laptop</summary>
1. Click the "Install Memoku" button at the top of the browser.

<img
  className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl'
  src='/images/contents/home/pwa_pc_chrome_1.webp'
  alt=''
/>

2.  Select "Install".

  <img
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl'
    src='/images/contents/home/pwa_pc_chrome_2.webp'
    alt=''
  />

3.  You can use it from your desktop.

  <img 
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl' 
    src='/images/contents/home/pwa_pc_chrome_3.webp' 
    alt='' 
  />
</details>

<details>
  <summary className='underline'>Android mobile</summary>

1.  Tap the share button at the bottom (or top) of the browser.

  <img
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl'
    src='/images/contents/home/pwa_mo_chrome_1.webp'
    alt=''
  />

2.  Select "Add to Home screen".

  <img
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl'
    src='/images/contents/home/pwa_mo_chrome_2.webp'
    alt=''
  />

3.  Confirm the name and finish adding.

  <img 
    className='w-full max-w-[500px] aspect-[5/3] rounded-xl' 
    src='/images/contents/home/pwa_mo_chrome_3.webp' 
    alt='' 
  />

</details>
